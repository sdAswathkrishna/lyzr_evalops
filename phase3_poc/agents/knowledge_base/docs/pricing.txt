ACME Platform — Pricing & Plans Guide
======================================

PLAN OVERVIEW
-------------

STARTER — $49/month (billed monthly) or $39/month (billed annually)
  Ideal for: Small teams and early-stage startups
  Users: Up to 3 seats
  Contacts: Up to 10,000
  Email sends: 10,000/month included
  Storage: 5 GB
  Support: Community forum + email (48h SLA)
  Key features:
    - CRM (contacts, deals, activities)
    - Basic analytics (pre-built reports only)
    - 5 active automations
    - 1 pipeline
    - CSV import/export
    - REST API access (1,000 req/min)
    - Zapier integration
    - 14-day free trial, no credit card required

GROWTH — $199/month (billed monthly) or $159/month (billed annually)
  Ideal for: Growing teams needing deeper insights and automation
  Users: Up to 25 seats (additional seats: $12/user/month)
  Contacts: Up to 100,000 (additional contacts: $20 per 10k/month)
  Email sends: 50,000/month included ($0.001 per additional email)
  SMS sends: 500/month included ($0.015 per additional SMS)
  Storage: 50 GB
  Support: Email + chat (8h SLA, Mon–Fri 9am–6pm local time)
  Key features:
    - Everything in Starter
    - Custom analytics dashboards (up to 20)
    - Advanced reports + custom report builder
    - Unlimited automations + sequences (email drip)
    - A/B testing for email sequences
    - Unlimited pipelines + custom fields (up to 50 per object)
    - Two-way calendar sync (Google + Outlook)
    - REST API access (5,000 req/min)
    - Native integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Stripe, etc.)
    - Audit logs (1 year retention)
    - Data export

ENTERPRISE — Custom pricing (contact sales@acme.io)
  Ideal for: Large organizations with compliance, security, and scale requirements
  Users: Unlimited
  Contacts: Unlimited
  Email sends: Unlimited
  Storage: Unlimited
  Support: 24/7 phone + dedicated Customer Success Manager + 1h critical SLA
  Key features:
    - Everything in Growth
    - SSO (SAML 2.0) with enforcement
    - Custom data residency (US / EU / APAC)
    - HIPAA-eligible add-on (requires BAA)
    - SOC 2 Type II + ISO 27001 compliance documentation
    - Audit logs (7 year retention)
    - Real-time analytics dashboards
    - Unlimited custom fields
    - Dedicated onboarding + implementation support
    - Custom SLA agreements
    - API rate limit: 20,000 req/min
    - Priority feature requests
    - Custom contract terms and invoicing

ADD-ONS (available on Growth and Enterprise)
--------------------------------------------
  - Extra seats: $12/user/month (Growth) or custom (Enterprise)
  - Extra contacts: $20 per 10,000 contacts/month
  - Email add-on: 100,000 additional emails/month for $80
  - SMS add-on: 5,000 additional SMS/month for $65
  - Analytics Pro: Advanced ML-based forecasting, $99/month (Growth only)
  - HIPAA add-on: Enables HIPAA data handling, $199/month (Enterprise only)
  - Data residency change: One-time migration fee of $500

BILLING DETAILS
---------------
  - Billing cycle: Monthly or annual (annual saves ~20%)
  - Payment methods: Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), ACH bank transfer (US only),
    wire transfer (Enterprise), purchase orders (Enterprise)
  - Invoices: Sent on the 1st of each month to the billing email
  - Failed payments: 3 retries over 7 days → account suspended → 14-day grace → delete
  - Upgrades: Take effect immediately; prorated charge for remaining cycle
  - Downgrades: Take effect at end of current billing cycle; no refund for prorated period
  - Annual plans: Paid upfront; no refunds after 30-day cancellation window

FREE TRIAL
----------
  - 14 days, all Growth features, no credit card required
  - At trial end: choose a plan, or account moves to read-only archive mode
  - Data retained for 30 days in archive, then deleted if no plan selected
  - Extend trial by 7 days: request once from Settings → Billing → Extend Trial

FREQUENTLY ASKED BILLING QUESTIONS
------------------------------------

Q: Can I switch from monthly to annual billing mid-cycle?
A: Yes. Settings → Billing → Change to Annual. You'll pay the prorated annual amount
   immediately minus any credit from unused monthly payments.

Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?
A: Your data is retained in a read-only archive for 30 days after cancellation. You can
   export it during this window. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted.

Q: Do you support purchase orders?
A: Yes, but only on Enterprise plans. Contact enterprise@acme.io with your PO number and
   billing requirements.

Q: Is the price per seat or per org?
A: Plans are priced per org (organization). Each plan includes a seat limit. Additional
   seats can be purchased as add-ons. One user = one seat.
