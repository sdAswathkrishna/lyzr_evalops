ACME Platform — Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
=================================================

ACCOUNT & LOGIN
---------------

Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
A: Go to app.acme.io/login and click "Forgot password". Enter your registered email address.
   You will receive a reset link valid for 60 minutes. If you don't see the email, check your
   spam folder or contact your admin if SSO is enforced on your account.

Q: My account is locked. What should I do?
A: Accounts lock after 5 consecutive failed login attempts. You can unlock via:
   1. The "Unlock account" link sent automatically to your registered email.
   2. Ask your org Admin to unlock from Settings → Team → select user → Unlock.
   3. Contact ACME support if neither option is available.

Q: Why does SSO login fail with "SAML assertion expired"?
A: This usually means your Identity Provider (IdP) clock is out of sync. ACME's SAML
   assertion validity window is 5 minutes. Ensure NTP is configured on your IdP server.
   If the problem persists, check that the SP metadata at app.acme.io/saml/metadata is
   correctly imported into your IdP.

Q: Can I use ACME on mobile?
A: Yes. ACME has iOS and Android apps (download from App Store / Google Play). The mobile
   app supports CRM, notifications, and dashboards. Automation and advanced analytics
   require the web interface.

Q: How do I enable MFA for my account?
A: Settings → My Account → Security → Enable MFA. Choose TOTP (recommended), SMS, or
   hardware key. Save your 8 backup codes in a secure place.

BILLING & PLANS
---------------

Q: How do I upgrade my plan?
A: Settings → Billing → Change Plan. Upgrades take effect immediately; you are charged a
   prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle.

Q: Can I get a refund if I downgrade?
A: Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. We do not offer
   prorated refunds for downgrades. Enterprise contracts may have custom refund terms.

Q: What happens if my payment fails?
A: ACME retries your payment 3 times over 7 days. After all retries fail, your account
   is suspended (read-only). You have a 14-day grace period to update your payment method
   before the account is permanently deleted.

Q: Do you offer a free trial?
A: Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. At the end of the
   trial you choose a plan or your account reverts to a read-only archive state.

Q: Is there a non-profit or startup discount?
A: Yes. Non-profits with 501(c)(3) status get 40% off Growth. Startups in recognized
   accelerators (Y Combinator, Techstars, etc.) get 6 months free on Growth.
   Apply at acme.io/discounts.

DATA & PRIVACY
--------------

Q: Where is my data stored?
A: By default, data is stored in the US (AWS us-east-1). EU storage (AWS eu-west-1) and
   APAC storage (AWS ap-southeast-1) are available on Enterprise plans.

Q: How do I export all my data?
A: Settings → Data & Privacy → Export Data. You will receive a download link via email
   within 2 hours. Export includes all contacts, deals, activities, and reports as JSON + CSV.

Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Only the Owner can delete an org. Settings → Billing → Delete Organization. There is
   a 30-day recovery window. After 30 days, all data is permanently destroyed (certified
   deletion report available on request for Enterprise).

Q: Is ACME HIPAA compliant?
A: ACME is HIPAA-eligible on Enterprise plans with a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA).
   Contact enterprise@acme.io to request a BAA. Healthcare data must be stored in the US
   data residency region.

INTEGRATIONS
------------

Q: How do I connect Salesforce?
A: Integrations → Salesforce → Connect. You need Salesforce Admin credentials. ACME
   installs a managed package in your Salesforce org for bidirectional sync. Initial
   sync may take 1-4 hours depending on data volume.

Q: The Slack integration stopped sending notifications. How do I fix it?
A: Most likely the Slack bot was removed from the channel or the workspace token was
   revoked. Go to Integrations → Slack → Reconnect, then re-add the ACME bot to your
   desired Slack channel. Also check that notifications are enabled for the workflow
   that should send to Slack (Automation → select workflow → Notify Slack action → verify channel).

Q: What is the API rate limit?
A: Rate limits by plan:
   - Starter: 1,000 requests/minute
   - Growth: 5,000 requests/minute
   - Enterprise: 20,000 requests/minute
   If you exceed the limit, you receive HTTP 429 with a Retry-After header. Consider
   batching API calls or upgrading your plan if you consistently hit limits.

ANALYTICS & REPORTS
-------------------

Q: My dashboard is showing stale data. How do I force a refresh?
A: Click the refresh icon (↻) on the top right of any dashboard. You can also change
   the refresh interval: Dashboard settings → Refresh interval. Real-time refresh is
   available on Enterprise plans only.

Q: Can I share a report with someone outside my org?
A: Yes. On any report, click Share → Public Link. The link is read-only and doesn't
   require login. You can set an expiry date (7 days, 30 days, never). Disable public
   links at any time from the Share menu.

Q: How long is data retained?
A: Event-level data: 12 months (Starter/Growth), 36 months (Enterprise).
   Aggregated/report data: unlimited on all plans.

TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------

Q: My CSV import failed. What went wrong?
A: Common causes:
   1. Missing required columns (email is required for contacts).
   2. Duplicate email addresses in the file.
   3. File exceeds 50MB size limit (split into smaller files).
   4. Special characters in headers — use only letters, numbers, and underscores.
   Download the error report from Imports → Failed → Download Error Log for row-level details.

Q: Emails sent from sequences are going to spam. What can I do?
A: 1. Verify your domain at Settings → Email → Domain Verification (add SPF, DKIM, DMARC records).
   2. Warm up your sending domain gradually — don't blast 10k emails on day one.
   3. Reduce the use of spam-trigger words and all-caps in subject lines.
   4. Ensure your unsubscribe link is prominent.
   5. Check your domain reputation at mail-tester.com.
