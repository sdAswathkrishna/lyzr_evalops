ACME Platform — Policies & Terms of Service Summary
=====================================================

This document summarizes key policies. Full legal documents at acme.io/legal.

DATA RETENTION POLICY
----------------------
User-Generated Data:
  - Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely while account is active.
  - Cancelled accounts: Data retained 30 days in read-only archive after cancellation.
  - Deleted accounts: Permanent deletion within 60 days of the 30-day archive window.
  - Deletion certificates available for Enterprise customers on request.

Event & Analytics Data:
  - Raw event data: 12 months (Starter/Growth), 36 months (Enterprise).
  - Aggregated report data: Retained indefinitely on all plans.
  - Audit logs: None (Starter), 1 year (Growth), 7 years (Enterprise).

Backup Policy:
  - Full database backups: Nightly, retained 30 days.
  - Point-in-time recovery: Available on Enterprise, up to 30 days lookback.
  - Backups are encrypted at rest (AES-256) and stored in a separate AWS region.

DATA PROCESSING & PRIVACY (GDPR/CCPA)
---------------------------------------
ACME acts as a Data Processor when handling personal data on behalf of customers.
Customers are the Data Controllers responsible for their end-users' data.

GDPR Compliance:
  - Data Processing Agreement (DPA) available at acme.io/legal/dpa.
  - Lawful basis: Customers must ensure they have a lawful basis for processing data in ACME.
  - Data Subject Requests (DSR): ACME provides tools for Customers to fulfill DSRs
    (export, delete) via the Data & Privacy settings page.
  - Data transfer: Personal data transferred to the US is protected by Standard
    Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

CCPA Compliance:
  - ACME does not sell Customer data to third parties.
  - California residents can request data deletion or access through their ACME admin.

Cookie Policy:
  - ACME uses strictly necessary cookies (session management, CSRF protection) and
    analytics cookies (can be disabled). No advertising cookies are used.

ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY (AUP)
-----------------------------
Prohibited uses of the ACME Platform:
  1. Sending unsolicited bulk email (spam) or SMS to people who have not opted in.
  2. Storing sensitive personal data beyond what is needed for your stated business purpose.
  3. Using ACME to conduct phishing attacks, credential harvesting, or fraud.
  4. Attempting to reverse-engineer, decompile, or disassemble ACME software.
  5. Sharing login credentials between multiple users (each person must have their own seat).
  6. Using automation workflows to artificially inflate activity metrics or gaming SLAs.
  7. Accessing another customer's data without authorization.

Violations may result in immediate account suspension without refund.

SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
-------------------------------
Uptime Commitments:
  - Starter: Best-effort, no formal SLA.
  - Growth: 99.5% monthly uptime SLA.
  - Enterprise: 99.9% monthly uptime SLA (critical components); custom SLAs available.

Downtime Definition:
  - Downtime = period when ACME core services are inaccessible from the public internet,
    excluding scheduled maintenance and events outside ACME's reasonable control.

Scheduled Maintenance:
  - Announced at least 48 hours in advance via status.acme.io and email.
  - Typically performed on Tuesday or Wednesday 2am–4am UTC.
  - Scheduled maintenance does not count toward downtime calculations.

SLA Credits:
  Uptime in Month   | Service Credit
  ≥ 99.9%           | No credit
  99.0% – 99.9%     | 10% of monthly invoice
  95.0% – 99.0%     | 25% of monthly invoice
  < 95.0%           | 50% of monthly invoice

  Credits are applied to the next invoice, not paid as cash. Must be claimed within 30
  days of the incident by submitting a request to support@acme.io.

SUPPORT POLICY
--------------
Support Tiers:
  - Community (Starter): Access to community.acme.io forums. No guaranteed response time.
  - Email (Starter): support@acme.io. 48-hour response SLA (business days).
  - Chat + Email (Growth): In-app chat + email. 8-hour SLA, Mon–Fri 9am–6pm customer's
    local time zone.
  - Phone + Chat + Email (Enterprise): 24/7 phone support + dedicated CSM.
    Critical issues: 1-hour response SLA.

Issue Severity Levels (Enterprise):
  P1 - Critical: System down, data loss risk. 1h response, 4h resolution target.
  P2 - High: Core feature unavailable, no workaround. 4h response, 8h resolution target.
  P3 - Medium: Feature degraded, workaround available. Next business day response.
  P4 - Low: Questions, feature requests, minor issues. 3 business days response.

Out-of-Scope Support:
  - Custom code or third-party integrations not built by ACME.
  - Issues caused by customer modifications to ACME configuration contrary to guidelines.
  - Infrastructure issues on the customer's side (ISP outages, firewalls blocking ACME IPs).

SECURITY POLICY
---------------
Penetration Testing:
  - Customers may conduct security testing on their own ACME tenants with prior written
    approval. Submit requests 5 business days in advance to security@acme.io.
  - DoS testing, testing other customers' tenants, and social engineering of ACME staff
    are strictly prohibited.

Vulnerability Disclosure:
  - ACME operates a responsible disclosure program.
  - Report security issues to security@acme.io. Response within 24 hours.
  - Bug bounty details at acme.io/security/bugbounty.

Incident Response:
  - In the event of a data breach affecting customer data, ACME will notify affected
    customers within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach (GDPR Article 33 requirement).
  - Notification includes: nature of breach, data types affected, mitigation steps taken.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
----------------------
  - ACME Platform, including all software, UI, and documentation, is owned by ACME Inc.
  - Customers retain ownership of all data they input into the platform.
  - ACME may use aggregated, anonymized usage data to improve the platform.
  - ACME name, logo, and trademarks may not be used without written permission.

TERMINATION & CANCELLATION
----------------------------
  - Customers may cancel at any time from Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription.
  - Monthly subscriptions: cancel before next billing date to avoid charge.
  - Annual subscriptions: cancel within 30 days for a prorated refund; no refund after 30 days.
  - ACME may terminate accounts that violate the AUP without notice.
  - Upon termination, 30-day data export window begins immediately.

CHANGES TO POLICIES
--------------------
  - ACME may update these policies with 30 days notice for material changes.
  - Notice sent via email to account owners and posted at acme.io/legal/changelog.
  - Continued use of ACME after the effective date constitutes acceptance of changes.
  - For Enterprise customers with custom contracts, contract terms supersede these policies
    where they conflict.
